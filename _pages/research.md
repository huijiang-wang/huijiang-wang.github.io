<h2 class="hj-section-title">Intelligent Soft Robotics</h2>

<div class="hj-research-project">
  <img src="{{ '/assets/img/research/soft-robotics.svg' | relative_url }}" alt="Dummy illustration for intelligent soft robotics">
  <div class="hj-research-project-body">
    <p>
      This theme explores how mechanical design, compliant materials, and embodied dynamics can create adaptive behavior without relying only on complex control. It connects variable-stiffness mechanisms, bio-inspired design, and experimentally grounded robot behavior.
    </p>

    <p class="hj-research-keywords"><strong>Keywords:</strong> embodied dynamics, soft robotics, variable stiffness, adaptive behavior, bio-inspired design</p>

<h2 class="hj-section-title">Soft Grippers and Resilient Manipulation</h2>

<div class="hj-research-project">
  <img src="{{ '/assets/img/research/soft-grippers.svg' | relative_url }}" alt="Dummy illustration for soft grippers and resilient manipulation">
  <div class="hj-research-project-body">
    <p>
      This project develops soft and form-flexible grippers for reliable interaction with objects of varied geometry and material properties. It includes self-closing suction grippers, self-healing grippers, and industrial studies of adaptable grasping.
    </p>

    <p class="hj-research-keywords"><strong>Keywords:</strong> soft grippers, suction grasping, self-healing materials, industrial manipulation, form-flexible design</p>

<h2 class="hj-section-title">Dexterous Robotic Hands and Expressive Manipulation</h2>

<div class="hj-research-project">
  <img src="{{ '/assets/img/research/dexterous-hands.svg' | relative_url }}" alt="Dummy illustration for dexterous robotic hands">
  <div class="hj-research-project-body">
    <p>
      This theme studies anthropomorphic hands and expressive manipulation, using piano playing as a demanding test case. The work spans hand design, passive properties, variable stiffness, data-driven simulation, and neuro-musculoskeletal modeling.
    </p>

    <p class="hj-research-keywords"><strong>Keywords:</strong> dexterous manipulation, anthropomorphic hands, piano-playing robots, passive dynamics, neuro-musculoskeletal modeling</p>

<h2 class="hj-section-title">Robotic Sensing and Embodied Intelligence</h2>

<div class="hj-research-project">
  <img src="{{ '/assets/img/research/robotic-sensing.svg' | relative_url }}" alt="Dummy illustration for robotic sensing and embodied intelligence">
  <div class="hj-research-project-body">
    <p>
      This work connects physical deformation, material response, and environmental interaction with usable robot state information. It treats sensing as part of the robot body, supporting designs where structure, perception, and actuation are developed together.
    </p>

    <p class="hj-research-keywords"><strong>Keywords:</strong> soft-robot sensing, touchless sensing, fibre-optic sensors, integrated perception, material intelligence</p>

<h2 class="hj-section-title">Human-Robot Collaboration and Learning</h2>

<div class="hj-research-project">
  <img src="{{ '/assets/img/research/human-robot-collaboration.svg' | relative_url }}" alt="Dummy illustration for human-robot collaboration and learning">
  <div class="hj-research-project-body">
    <p>
      This project explores collaborative behavior in tasks where robots must respond to people in real time. It includes learning-based piano accompaniment, emotional alignment in musical tasks, and low-latency robot-assisted hand-object interaction.
    </p>

    <p class="hj-research-keywords"><strong>Keywords:</strong> human-robot collaboration, real-time accompaniment, robot learning, hand-object interaction, musical robotics</p>
